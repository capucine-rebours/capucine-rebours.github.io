My teaching experience spans undergraduate and graduate levels in clinical 
psychology, neuropsychology, and developmental psychology. I place particular 
emphasis on neurodiversity-affirmative frameworks and have developed original 
course content on gender and autism at both undergraduate and graduate levels.

## Professional Training

I deliver specialized training sessions on social camouflaging and autistic 
burnout for clinicians and care professionals working with autistic individuals, 
developed in collaboration with local autistic communities.
